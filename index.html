CIIT Senior High School

Since K12 was implemented, it was a goal for students to master concepts and skills of their desired field and develop those concepts for lifelong learning. The goal of K12 is to prepare the youth for tertiary education, skills, and development, employment, and entrepreneurship.


Therefore, as one of the country's top institutions for digital and technical education, CIIT Philippines offers a Senior High School program that prepares to adapt to the changing expectations of the industry. The school created an industry-standard curriculum that includes hands-on learning in multimedia, animation, programming, and provides information and abilities that students may apply even before entering college. The Senior High curriculum includes specialized subjects that can be credited to college.


WHAT ARE THE TRACKS AND STRANDS CIIT HAS TO OFFER?

Programming

Get hands-on experience with real-world programming even in Senior High School. Immerse yourself in a unique industry-ready curriculum focusing on developing the students' knowledge and skills in the current methods of software development and design, with professional and executive teachers.
CIIT SHS is the first Senior High School in the country that adapts the CODE.ORG Curriculum, developed by professional engineers in Google, Youtube, Amazon, Microsoft, Facebook, and Twitter. This curriculum makes computer science and programming skills for accessible for the youth. Together with Preparation for Philippine Programmers Rating (PPR), the first automated assessment tool aligned with the needs of the Philippine software industry, to identify entry-level programming talent and skills in the country.

Introduction to Computing System Analyst
Computer Programming 1 and 2 System Programmer

Information Management Graphics Programmer
Object Oriented Programming Senior interactive Software Developer
Introduction to Game Design and Development Game Designer
